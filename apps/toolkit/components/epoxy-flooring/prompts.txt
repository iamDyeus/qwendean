### 9. FAQ

**Purpose:** Handle objections without a sales call. B2G procurement has specific questions around compliance, timelines, and documentation.

```
Create a FAQSection React component for an industrial epoxy flooring company targeting government and commercial procurement.

Tech: Typescript, TailwindCSS, ShadCN UI, lucide-react icons (use ShadCN Accordion component).

Content to include:
- Section headline: "Questions Procurement Teams Usually Ask"
- 7 FAQ items using ShadCN Accordion, with specific Q&A content:
    Q1: "Do your floors meet IS 15477 and FSSAI standards?" — A: "Yes. Our epoxy systems are specified to meet IS 15477 for industrial flooring and are compliant with FSSAI hygiene requirements for food-grade facilities. Compliance documentation is provided at handover."
    Q2: "How long does installation take for a large facility?" — A: "For facilities up to 10,000 sq ft, installation typically takes 3–5 working days with 24–48 hour cure time. Larger projects are phased to keep sections of your facility operational."
    Q3: "Can you work around our operating schedule?" — A: "Yes. We regularly execute night shifts and weekend work for facilities that can't afford daytime shutdowns — depots, warehouses, and food units especially."
    Q4: "What surface preparation do you do before applying epoxy?" — A: "We mechanically grind or shot-blast the existing surface to open the concrete profile, repair cracks, and ensure bond strength. This step is non-negotiable for a durable coat."
    Q5: "Do you provide warranty documentation suitable for audit files?" — A: "Yes. Every project includes a written warranty, material test certificates, and application records formatted for compliance audit submission."
    Q6: "What is the minimum order or project size you work on?" — A: "We work on projects from 1,000 sq ft upward. For government tenders, we can provide all required documentation including GST invoices, PAN, and experience certificates."
    Q7: "How do we get a quote for a government tender project?" — A: "Call us or submit the contact form with your facility type and approximate area. We'll schedule a site visit and provide a written quotation with full specifications within 3 working days."

Layout: Single column accordion, full width, max-w-3xl centered
Style: Clean white background, subtle border on accordion items, amber chevron icon on expand, professional and document-like — not glossy
Export the component as: export { FAQSection }
```

## Build Order Recommendation

Start with **Navbar → Hero → SocialProofBar** first — that's your above-the-fold, and it'll set the visual tone for every section below. Then build **PainSection → FeaturesSection → HowItWorks** as the logical argument core. Close with **FAQ → Contact → Footer**.
